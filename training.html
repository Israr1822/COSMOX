Astronaut Training Program

The COSMOS Astronaut Training Program prepares you for a safe and exciting journey into space. Our comprehensive program helps you adapt to zero gravity, learn spacecraft procedures, and gain confidence for your mission. Train like an astronaut and prepare for the journey of a lifetime.

Train Like an Astronaut

Pre-Flight Schedule

Travelers follow a well-structured pre-flight plan that includes mission briefings, safety guidelines, equipment handling, and daily preparation tasks to build confidence before launch. This foundational phase sets the stage for your space journey.

Duration: 2 Weeks
2

Health & Fitness Requirements

All participants complete essential medical checks and light fitness assessments to ensure their bodies can handle zero-gravity and the physical demands of space travel. Our medical team ensures you're physically prepared for the experience.

Duration: 3 Days
3

Virtual Training Modules

Interactive VR modules introduce travelers to the spacecraft interior, emergency procedures, communication systems, and mission routines. Experience the spacecraft virtually before you board the real thing.

Duration: 5 Sessions
4

Simulated Zero-Gravity

Through controlled zero-gravity sessions in our specialized aircraft, travelers learn how to move, float, and coordinate in weightless conditions similar to real space environments. Master weightlessness before your flight.

Duration: 2 Flights
5

Meet the Instructors

Participants meet and train with certified astronaut instructors who share professional advice, space-travel techniques, and real mission experiences. Learn from those who have been where you're going.

Duration: Ongoing Support
