AstroAnswers – Quick Solutions

How do I reschedule my training session?
Training sessions can be rescheduled through your Booking Portal up to 48 hours before the scheduled start time without penalty. Visit your dashboard for available slots.
What medical documents are required?
A full AeroCert Class 3 medical clearance is required. You can upload these documents directly to the HealthCheck Portal or bring them to your pre-flight medical evaluation.
Is spaceflight insurance mandatory?
Yes, basic CosmoProtect coverage is included in your ticket price. Additional comprehensive coverage options are available during checkout for enhanced protection.
Can family members watch the launch?
Absolutely. Every astronaut ticket includes access for up to 4 guests at our VIP launch viewing gallery at the spaceport. Additional passes can be purchased.
What is the refund policy?
Missions are fully refundable up to 90 days before launch. Between 90 and 30 days, a 50% refund is available. Within 30 days, special arrangements may apply. Please review your specific ticket terms for details.
